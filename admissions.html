Admission Process

  1. Registration: Fill out the admission form available at the school office or download from our website.
  2. Document Submission: Submit the filled form along with the required documents (see below).
  3. Interaction/Entrance Test: For certain classes, an interaction or entrance test may be conducted.
  4. Admission Confirmation: Selected students will be notified. Complete the fee payment to confirm admission.

Required Documents

  • Birth Certificate (for Nursery & Class 1)
  • Previous School Transfer Certificate (if applicable)
  • Marksheet/Report Card of last class attended
  • Passport size photographs (2 copies)
  • Aadhaar Card (Student & Parents)
  • Address Proof

Important Dates

  • Admission forms available: 1st October 2025
  • Last date for form submission: 31st October 2025
  • Entrance/Interaction (if any): First week of November 2025
  • Session begins: April 2026
